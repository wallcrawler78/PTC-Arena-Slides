üí° How to Use

Basic Workflow

1. Login to Arena with your credentials
2. Search for items you want to present
3. Select items from search results
4. Generate slides by clicking "Generate from Selection"
5. Describe your presentation intent (e.g., "Executive summary for Q4 review")
6. Review the AI-generated slides in your presentation

Advanced: Collections

Collections let you combine items from multiple searches:

  • Search for ECOs related to a project
  • Click "Add to Collection" to save them
  • Search for affected Items and add to the same collection
  • Click "üíæ Save" to name and store your collection
  • Generate slides from the entire collection
  • Load saved collections anytime from "Manage Collections"
